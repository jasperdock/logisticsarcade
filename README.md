# Logistics Arcade

Freight-inspired arcade games where operational chaos becomes high scores.

## About
Logistics Arcade is a collection of browser-based games inspired by real-world freight forwarding, supply chain operations, warehousing, transportation, customs compliance and logistics planning.

The goal is simple:
Turn everyday logistics challenges into fun, replayable arcade experiences.
While the games take inspiration from real industry concepts, they are designed to be accessible to anyone — whether you're a logistics professional, a gamer, or simply someone who enjoys solving problems under pressure.

---

## Current Games

### Container Stuffer
A container loading puzzle where every square metre matters.
Load cargo safely, maximise revenue, manage cut-off times and avoid costly mistakes. Inspired by real container planning and freight optimisation.

### Container Stacker
A warehouse-style stacking challenge focused on space utilisation, cargo positioning and operational efficiency.

### Cargo Run
Collect, store and deliver cargo while managing warehouse capacity, perishables, dangerous goods, inspections and operational disruptions.
Revenue optimisation disguised as an arcade game.

## Design Philosophy

Logistics is often viewed as invisible when everything works.
In reality, it is one of the most complex, fast-moving and problem-solving-driven industries in the world.
Logistics Arcade aims to capture that feeling:

* Operational pressure
* Time-critical decisions
* Resource management
* Trade-offs
* Optimisation
* Occasional chaos

And turn it into gameplay.
